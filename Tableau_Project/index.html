About the Analysis

India's electricity consumption and production have been crucial metrics to understand its economic growth and energy needs. Here are some key points about India's electricity consumption during the period 2019-2020

  • Total Consumption: India's total electricity consumption in the fiscal year 2019-2020 was approximately 1,181 TWh (terawatt-hours).
  • Electricity Production: In the fiscal year 2019-2020, India's electricity generation was approximately 1,383 TWh.
  • Renewable Energy Growth: By the end of 2019-2020, the installed capacity for renewable energy reached around 87 GW (gigawatts).

Various government initiatives aimed at improving energy efficiency, reducing losses, and increasing the share of renewable energy in the energy mix were in place. Schemes like the Ujwal DISCOM Assurance Yojana (UDAY) aimed to improve the financial and operational efficiency of power distribution companies.
